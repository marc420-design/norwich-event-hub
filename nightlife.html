Norwich Nightlife & Club Nights

Norwich is known for its thriving nightlife scene, with a rich history of drum & bass, techno, and underground electronic music. From legendary venues like The Waterfront and Epic Studios to intimate basement clubs like The Birdcage, Norwich offers something for every taste.

Whether you're into heavy basslines, commercial club anthems, or underground warehouse raves, you'll find your tribe here. Our nightlife calendar features everything from student nights and retro throwbacks to cutting-edge electronic showcases and international DJ bookings.

Popular Nightlife Venues in Norwich

  • The Waterfront - Live music and club nights
  • Epic Studios - Underground electronic music
  • The Birdcage - Indie and alternative nights
  • The Loft - Commercial club nights
