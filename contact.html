Frequently Asked Questions

How do I submit an event?

Use our Submit Event form. It's free and takes just a few minutes. We'll review and publish approved events within 24 hours.

How much does it cost to list an event?

It's completely free! Norwich Event Hub is a free platform for event organizers and attendees.

Can I edit my event after submission?

Yes! Email us at submit@norwicheventshub.com with your event ID and the changes you need.

How do I report an issue with a listing?

If you spot incorrect information or a problematic listing, please email info@norwicheventshub.com with details.

Can I partner with Norwich Event Hub?

We're always open to partnerships that benefit the Norwich events community. Contact us at press@norwicheventshub.com

Do you have an API for developers?

We're working on a public API for developers. Stay tuned or email info@norwicheventshub.com to express interest.
