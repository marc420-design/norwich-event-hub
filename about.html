About Norwich Event Hub

Your independent guide to Norwich events, nightlife and culture

Who We Are

Norwich Event Hub is an independent platform dedicated to showcasing the best of Norwich's vibrant events scene. From underground gigs and club nights to theatre productions, markets, and free community events — we're here to make sure you never miss out.

We're not affiliated with any council, venue, or promoter. We're simply passionate about Norwich's cultural scene and want to make it easier for everyone to discover what's happening in this amazing city.

Our Mission

Our goal is simple: to be the most comprehensive, up-to-date, and easy-to-use events guide for Norwich. Whether you're looking for:

  • Tonight's club nights and DJ sets
  • This weekend's live music and gigs
  • Upcoming theatre and cultural events
  • Free community gatherings and markets
  • Sports events and outdoor activities

...we've got you covered.
